AIM 1.0

AIM 1.0 is an innovative entrepreneurial ideas enhancement program envisioned by IIC. Its mission is to motivate learners about brainstorming entrepreneurial thinking and ideas for providing solutions to the problems faced by one in daily lives and promote the same ideology to seed the birth of future entrepreneurs. The purpose of AIM 1.0 is to come together for organized idea generation exercises and to help individuals and teams to uncover revolutionary ideas and to think boldly about the future. Its an event providing a common platform for all to present their ideas to the fellow professionals and entrepreneurs for assistance and guidance for bringing their idea true in reality.







GUIDELINES

1. The participants are required to form a team of 2 to 3 members and should get them registered latest by 23rd November of 2021.

2. The teams have to come up with an innovative idea that either solves any of the real world problems or possess a potential of bringing the revolution in any domain of services.

3. The teams have to draft a presentation showcasing their idea and submit it latest by 11 a.m. of 25th November of 2021.

4. The presentations will be shortlisted by the officials.

5. The selected teams will be then qualified to present their ideas infront of the officials and industry professionals on the event at 26th November of 2021.

6. Every team who submits their idea will be awarded with a certificate as a token of participation while the shortlisted teams will be awarded with certificates as a token of appreciation. The winning teams will be awarded with gifts and certificates and will also be granted with some resources under some terms and conditions for pursuing and bringing their idea to reality.

ALL THE VERY BEST!
